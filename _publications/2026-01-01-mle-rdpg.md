We study maximum likelihood estimation (MLE) for the Random Dot Product Graph (RDPG) model when only a subset of edges are observed. We establish convergence guarantees under a projected-gradient / Newton framework, propose an out-of-sample extension for link prediction on new nodes, and demonstrate state-of-the-art performance on several real-world network benchmarks.
