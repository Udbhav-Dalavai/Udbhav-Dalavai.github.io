We derive and study a parametric family of link functions for the Conway-Maxwell-Poisson (COM-Poisson) regression model. The proposed link accommodates both over- and under-dispersed count data and nests standard choices (log, logit) as special cases.

*Under review at the Journal of Applied Statistics.*
